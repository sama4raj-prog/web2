What is neuroscience?

Neuroscience is the scientific study of the nervous system (the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nervous system), its functions, and its disorders.

What is cognitive neuroscience?

Cognitive neuroscience is the scientific field bridging psychology and neurobiology that investigates how the physical brain enables the mind, memory, attention, language, and emotion.

What is developmental neuroscience?

Developmental neuroscience is the study of how the nervous system forms, grows, and changes across the lifespan, from embryonic development through adulthood.
